Flameshot:
	Make Flameshot the Default Screenshot Tool
		1. Click the Kali "Start Menu" --> "Keyboard"
		2. Click the "Application Shortcuts" Tab
		3. Locate the command "xfce4-screenshooter" associated to "Print"
			a. Select it --> Click the "--- Remove" Button
			b. Click the "+ Add" Button
			c. In the "Command:" box, enter-in: "/usr/bin/flameshot gui"
			d. Click "OK"
			e. In the "Command Shortcut" window, press the "Print Screen" button
		---FIN---
